Plain Vanilla Cake

Recipe for the CAKEEE!

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour, spooned into measuring cup and leveled-off with back edge of knife
  2. 2 teaspoons baking powder
  3. ¼ teaspoon salt
  4. 12 tablespoons (1½ sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  5. 1½ cups granulated sugar
  6. 6 large egg whites, at room temperature
  7. ¾ cup whole milk, at room temperature
  8. 1½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  9. ½ teaspoon almond extract
